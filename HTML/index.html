kiinMedia is an independent video production company specializing in documentaries that promote the work of mission driven organizations and people working for cultural arts, advocacy and educational causes.

Producer of creative projects in mission driven media and video supporting the arts, humanities and communities working for positive social change, Mati Kiin is a videographer located in western Massachusetts. With a background in media production and non-profit development, Mati is honing the art of telling your story. When presented with an idea, project, or goal, Mati provides a media outlet for demonstration, expression, or education through video.
